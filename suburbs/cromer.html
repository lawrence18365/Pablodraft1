Serving Cromer’s Diverse Community

Cromer is home to 8,030 residents with a median age of 41 and a strong family presence. Homes here are predominantly separate houses (78.1%) with a relatively low rental share (15.0%), and a median weekly household income of $2,483. English is spoken at home by 83.6% of residents, with Italian, Mandarin and Portuguese among the top additional languages.

Our heritage‑conscious approach respects Cromer’s story: originally known as Dee Why West, the suburb took its current name in the 1960s (after the local golf club adopted “Cromer”) and grew from semi‑rural bush and farms into today’s leafy residential haven.

Understanding Cromer’s Unique Cleaning Challenges

Cromer sits about 20 km north‑east of the Sydney CBD, bounded by bushland and the shores of Narrabeen Lagoon. That mix of salt air, lagoon humidity, bush pollen and fine grit can shorten the life of carpets, rugs and upholstery if residues are left behind.

Our eco‑friendly, wool‑safe, soap‑free chemistry and multi‑step heated extraction are designed for these conditions — protecting premium textiles while keeping indoor air crisp and fresh.

Location & Accessibility

Strategically positioned between Wakehurst Parkway and Pittwater Road, Cromer has rapid links across the Northern Beaches. Fast public transport is close by via the B‑Line (B1) at Dee Why/Brookvale, running frequently to Wynyard; many residents connect to the B‑Line and reach the CBD in around 35–40 minutes.

Protecting High‑Value Properties

Cromer’s real estate market features premium family homes and quality apartments. Recent medians: houses around $2,302,250 and units about $795,000. Our factory‑grade fabric protection and specialist natural‑fibre techniques help preserve value in high‑end furnishings throughout the home.

A Deeper Dive into the Cromer Community

Roughly 20 km from the CBD and ~6.3 km² in area, Cromer blends bushland and lagoon living with easy access to outdoor icons: the 8.6 km Narrabeen Lagoon Trail, Cromer Golf Club beside the lagoon and Garigal National Park, and Cromer Park — home of Manly United FC.

Families are well served by Cromer Public School and NBSC Cromer Campus (Years 7–12). We’re proud to offer our “eco‑friendly upholstery cleaning” and “professional carpet cleaners Northern Beaches” services to this active, outdoors‑loving community.
